Entities In HTML

A HTML entity is an expression that begins with &, and ends with ;. The major roles of entities are:

  • Displaying Reserved Characters.

  • Printing Invisible Characters.

  • Printing Untypeable Characters.

Every HTML entity is rendered differently, by different browsers.
